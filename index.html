77th Army Day Celebrations in Pune

The 77th Army Day was celebrated on January 15, 2025, in Pune for the first time, marking Field Marshal KM Cariappa’s appointment as the first Indian Commander-in-Chief in 1949. The event showcased 100 robotic dogs, advanced military equipment, and a grand parade under the Southern Command. An investiture ceremony honored gallantry awardees, highlighting the Army’s commitment to innovation and modernization. The celebrations emphasized self-reliance, technological advancements, and women’s inclusion in the forces.
